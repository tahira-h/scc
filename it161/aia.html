Audiences Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach
Employers
  • Contact information for Jessie.
  • Needs to be able to quickly review my client's projects in a portfolio.
  • Want to learn about my client to understand why is this person the best choice for their company.
  • Provide a contact page to message Jessie.
  • Make the website accessible to review with an great control flow for the user's journey on the website.
  • Clearly provide an About Me page.
Recruiters
  • Looking to match my client with a company or third party who has an open position.
  • Needs a way to review my client's projects in a portfolio to market to employers.
  • Want to learn about my client to successfully schedule an interview with a company that this person qualifies for.
  • Clearly present a way for a recruiter to message my client to assist with matching a company who has an open position.
  • Publish an accessible website for the recruiter to view my client's portfolio.
  • Make it clear the companies my client is interested in to guide the recruiter to specific companies and similar companies.
Students
  • Need to find a website to visually assess what their audience is looking for.
  • Researching designers who are in the same pool of prospects to validate their idea of a product.
  • Looking for a way to be a better designer in their career field.
  • Provide a website that provides limited jargon language.
  • Promote a website that uses positive language to encourage others that are in the same pool of prospects.
  • Clearly provide social media links for students to find various platforms to research what they are looking for.
