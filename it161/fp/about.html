My Story

I am currently an artist that specializes in videography with the use of drone footage, along with creating abstract backgrounds for events like concerts and local band groups. I have started in my field since 2023 and open to working more projects with clients.

Attending Sacramento State has been a privilege, from the first moment after graduating high school. When I first decided to take my first step into pursuing my education, my main priority was to immediately start searching for a college or university campus to attend right after high school no matter how far it may be. In addition I want my family history to recognize me as first generation to attend college at an early age and to be a prime example on how you can overcome your challenges to accomplish your dreams of attending a college.
